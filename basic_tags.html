Mahatma Gandhi Biography

  1. Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi[14] was born on 2 October 1869 into a Gujarati Hindu Modh Baniya family
  2. At age 9, Gandhi entered the local school in Rajkot, near his home.
  3. At age 11, he joined the High School in Rajkot
  4. His only companions were books and school lessons
  5. In May 1883, the 13-year-old Mohandas was married to 14-year-old Kasturbai Makhanji Kapadia

  • In India, he is also called as Bapu and also known as the Father of the Nation.
  • Gandhi first employed nonviolent civil disobedience as an expatriate lawyer in South Africa
  • After his return to India in 1915, he set about organising peasants, farmers, and urban labourers to protest against excessive land-tax and discrimination
